### Project Overview:

I analyzed a real-world marketing dataset from a Portuguese bank to predict whether customers would subscribe to a term deposit, given data from previous interactions and their current marketing campaign. I built and evaluated both a linear regression model to examine the relationship between client age and housing loan status, and a logistic regression classifier to predict subscription likelihood based on campaign history, job type, and prior  contact frequency.

**Bank Marketing Dataset: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/222/bank+marketing**
